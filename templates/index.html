Convert Scanned PDFs into Searchable PDFs

Tips and settings

🌐 Default language
?

The OCR engine defaults to kat. Switch to kat+eng for mixed Georgian and English pages.

🔑 Protected PDFs
Advanced

Desktop builds focus on the built-in auto unlock + OCR flow. The app tries a blank password first, removes restrictions when possible, and uses the password field only when the PDF needs a real user password.
